That time Eric went missing

We had just landed in Germany for a well needed break when we got the phonecall - Eric slipped his lead and ran off.

Jim’s hair is everywhere

Everywhere. No really, I’m not sure you understand. There isn’t a crevice in the house that hasn’t been cleaned and it’s still there. All the time.

Eric’s fake cough

No, it’s not allergies. No, it’s not something he ate. It’s not a hairball, a bone or piece of a toy. He’s faking it and it’s all to get out attention.

Tony came to visit
