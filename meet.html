Meet the Two Dogs

These two are pretty special

Both had rough starts in life but now live the doggy dream with their two pals Andrew and Louise.


Eric was rescued in 2015 suffering from severe neglect. He was in such bad condition the vet considered euthanasia. Luckily, Andrew met him and knew he could help Eric.


Jim was picked up off the side of a motorway with his mother and siblings in 2016. Only a few weeks old, the pups were starving and barely alive. Permanently rehoming huskies is a difficult task but luckily Andrew and Louise knew Jim would always be welcome.


These days they are both happy and healthy with daily runs, lots of play and too many treast!

About the breeds

Chinese Crested

Loveable and loyal, Chinese Crested can be either hairless or coated. They are a medium sized dog that are particularly good with children.

German Shepherd

A large dog, German Shepherds are hard-working and a fierce guard. Known for their loyalty, they are very intelligent and highly trainable.

Siberian Husky

Independent and stubborn, Huskies are known for their endless energy. They are gentle dogs that are often smaller than expected.
