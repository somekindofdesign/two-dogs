Meet the Two Dogs

These Two Dogs are pretty special. Both had rough starts in life but now live the doggy dream with their two pals Andrew and Louise.

Eric was the first to join the family. He was rescued in 2015 in a group of 18 dogs suffering from severe neglect. Being the runt, he was dominated by the others and was in such bad condition the vet considered euthanasia. Luckily, Andrew met him and knew he could help.

Jim came next. He was picked up off the side of a motorway with his mother, brother and sisters in 2016. Only a few weeks old, the pups were starving and barely alive. Huskies are a commonly abandoned breed so rehoming them was a difficult task but luckily Andrew and Louise knew Jim would always be welcome.

About the breeds

Chinese Crested

Loveable and loyal, Chinese Crested can be either hairless or coated. They are a medium sized dog that are particularly good with children.

German Shepherd

A large dog, German Shepherds are hard-working and a fierce guard. Known for their loyalty, they are very intelligent and highly trainable.

Siberian Husky

Independent and stubborn, Huskies are known for their endless energy. They are gentle dogs that are often smaller than expected.
