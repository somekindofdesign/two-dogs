Walking Two Dogs

PUBLISHED 24th August 2020

I'm done, I can't do it. Not the two dogs, not on my own.

They're just so different!

Jim want's to make friends with everyone, sniff everything and race down the road.

Eric tries to attack or run from everyone, stand under Jim when he lifts his leg and meander down the road.

This is only an issue when they're on the lead, when they're free to roam then there's no trouble. In fact, they seem to get on better!

I'm done though. Unless it's an emergency, I won't be walking the two dogs together on a lead.
