Jim has an enemy

August 15th 2022

Just one. The only living thing he has ever reacted negatively and of course, it's the dog next door.

Darcy is an elderly greyhound. He's vocal but very sweet and very friendly.

Since the day we moved in, over three years ago, Jim has hated him. His very existence gets to Baby Jim. We have no idea why.

It's pretty inconvenient but also pretty worrying. Poor Darcy wouldn't fare too well if Jim ever got to him. It's not a chance anyone wants to take.

That means Jim isn't allowed in the front garden, he has to be harnessed and on a lead to be brought out to the car.
It would be nice if Jim got over himself.
